### MWC (Mobile World Congress) - Barcelona

**Event Dates:** February 24-27, 2026
**Venue:** Fira Barcelona Gran Via
**Setup Period:** February 20-23, 2026
**Dismantling:** February 27-28, 2026

**Key Regulations:**
- **Height Restrictions:** 
  - Standard: 4m maximum
  - Special permission required for >4m
- **Fire Safety:**
  - All materials must be fire-retardant (M1 classification)
  - Fire extinguishers mandatory
- **Electrical:**
  - All installations by certified electricians
  - Maximum power: Check with organizer
- **Sound:**
  - Maximum 70dB
  - No sound after 18:00 during setup
- **Waste:**
  - Mandatory recycling
  - Separate waste collection points

**Important Deadlines:**
- Design submission: 60 days before event
- Technical plan approval: 45 days before
- Final stand plan: 30 days before
- Contractor registration: 21 days before

**Reference:** https://gsma.my.site.com/mwcoem/s/Stand%20Build%20Rules%20and%20Regulations
